Until The End Non-Profit Organisation


Until The End is a non profit organisation that is set out to help people who are unemployed or those struggling to finish their schools becaue of financial constrains as well as those who are eliminated by bursaries.

We have not have started very long ago but we are doing everything in our power to make sure that we are able to help in various locations.

We believe that in few years from now we would make a significant impact to many of our fellow brothers and sisters who are failing to provide for themselves.Because of this we want people not to have any excuses on why they aren't employed or why they aren't at school

That is our VISION and we will stand by it no matter what because God has priviledge us with enough resources that we can use to help one another so we will use them to change somebody's life who had lost hope in humanity.


May The Grace Of The Lord Be With All God's People.AMEN
